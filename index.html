Welcome to sanskrit club

The Sanskrit Club is an initiative to revive and promote the bedrock for the growth of a large part of the glorious Indian Civilisation, the Sanskrit language. Knowledge of unknown proportions resides in texts composed in this ancient language, be it the Vedas, the Puranas, the Tantras, or texts composed by other Indian saints and writers. We, as students of a premier technological institution of this country, have taken it upon ourselves to approach Sanskrit with a scientific temperament and bring back to the world all the genius, be it literary, technological, philosophical or scientific, that resides within the texts of this language.

Shastra Setu

An Invitation to Indian Knowledge Systems: A Technologist Perspective


Click here to register

Sanskrit Club of IIT Roorkee is organizing yet another mesmerizing event, in collaboration with MIT School of Vedic Science, Pune. The event, called Shastra Setu, is an opportunity for all our friends from science and engineering backgrounds to acquaint themselves with the Indian Shastras and Scientific Thinking.
The speaker, Amit Rao, is a Senior Consultant and Former Research Scientist at the National Centre for Software Technology (NCST, now part of CDAC). In his words,
“As a technologist with interest in Sanskrit, but hardly any background in Indian Shastras, when I began learning about the various Vedic Sciences, I was fascinated by its features - the breadth and depth of inquiry, the rigorous approach, and above all, the underlying unity of thought and purpose. In this short talk, I will attempt to share some of my insights about this, highlight interesting features of this ecosystem, and set the tone for the rest of the series, in an informative and interactive manner.”
