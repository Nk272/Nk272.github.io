    • Session - 2

    Understanding and Predicting Human Behaviour: An Indic Perspective


  • The session, titled ‘Understanding and Predicting Human Behaviour: An Indic Perspective’, will be hosted on Zoom, on Saturday, August 15, 2020.
  • The speaker, Sagar Sanjay Vidwans, is Assistant Professor of Integral Psychology, MIT School of Vedic Sciences
  • The talk will cover the fundamental concept of human behaviour from a Vedic perspective as well as a modern perspective. It will throw light on the structure of the mind, its components, and its functions. How Vedic Psychology looks at psychology as a science of human experience, will also be discussed. The application of Vedic psychology will be introduced, in the context of counselling and self-inquiry
