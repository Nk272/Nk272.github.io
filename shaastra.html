    Understanding and Predicting Human Behaviour- An Indic Perspective


    Sanskrit Club of IIT Roorkee is organizing yet another mesmerizing event, in collaboration with MIT School of Vedic Science, Pune. The event, called Shaastra Setu, is an opportunity for all our friends from science and engineering backgrounds to acquaint themselves with the Indian Shastras and Scientific Thinking.
    The speaker, Amit Rao, is a Senior Consultant and Former Research Scientist at the National Centre for Software Technology (NCST, now part of CDAC). In his words,
    “As a technologist with interest in Sanskrit, but hardly any background in Indian Shastras, when I began learning about the various Vedic Sciences, I was fascinated by its features - the breadth and depth of inquiry, the rigorous approach, and above all, the underlying unity of thought and purpose. In this short talk, I will attempt to share some of my insights about this, highlight interesting features of this ecosystem, and set the tone for the rest of the series, in an informative and interactive manner.”
