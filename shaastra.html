    Panini’s Ashtadhyayi: A Computational Marvel


  • The session, titled ‘Panini’s Ashtadhyayi: A Computational Marvel’, was hosted on Zoom, on Saturday, August 29, 2020
  • The speaker, Radhika Goel, is an IT Management Professional with over 20 years of experience in leadership roles in Fortune 20 companies
  • The talk will introduce Maharshi Panini’s work, "The Ashtadhyayi". Also,how the concepts laid out by Panini more than 2500 years ago are relevant in today’s cutting-edge technology age of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence. The topics covered by the talk include Vyakarana vs. grammar, Ashtadhyayi's structure, its object-oriented concepts, and insights for Natural Language Processing.

    • Session - 4

    A tour guide to Indic Knowledge Disciplines and their Contemporary Relevance


  • The session, titled ‘ A tour guide to Indic Knowledge Disciplines and their Contemporary Relevance’, was hosted on Zoom, on Saturday, September 12, 2020
  • Before a student curious about Indic knowledge can dive into a specific area of study, it helps to get a tour of the overall landscape, what its branches are, and how they are relevant to modern disciplines. This talk gives a birds-eye view of the taxonomy and organization of Indic Knowledge Disciplines and draws parallels with contemporary ones. The talk throws light on the original context of each Indic discipline, and its current or potential use in the contemporary world. It also helps one identify where to look for Indic sources / perspectives relevant to a modern discipline, say, psychology, logic, social sciences, computation etc.
